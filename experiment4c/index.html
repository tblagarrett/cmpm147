Experiment 4c - Infinite Worlds

Description

This infinite world is made up of a bunch of stars that flash on and off randomly. I found it enjoyable to look at, and not much deeper than that. Exploring around you will see new stars show up and disappear.

Technical

This is done by manipulating the clicks function provided in the base. giving each drawn square a starting number of clicks, we add to it after a random amount of time. This creates an effect where the starts flicker on and off. I kept the effect slow, having it update every second instead of every frame.

Reflection

I would call this one a sequence of failures stumbling to something that looks alright. It has a big problem with optimization, being a little boring, and failure to execute on my ideas. But honestly, I think that's okay since I liked the other two so much. I spent a whole day doing the others, and couldn't come up with a good idea for this one that I was interested in. Such is life!
